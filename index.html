About Me

Music Producer

In the realm of music production, I have honed my skills to a fine point, specializing in the mesmerizing genres of R&B and trap. The groove of R&B and the edge of trap allow me to explore the full spectrum of emotions, creating tracks that resonate deeply with both heart and soul. But my sonic ventures don't stop there – I'm always eager to dive into uncharted territories, experimenting with a multitude of genres to push the boundaries of musical expression.
As a mixing engineer, I possess a meticulous attention to detail that ensures every beat, melody, and rhythm comes together seamlessly. The art of mixing is where science meets creativity, and I pride myself on striking that delicate balance to give each track its own unique character while maintaining a signature touch that's unmistakably Trippy Moksh.
But that's not all – beyond the studio, you'll find me behind the decks as a DJ, curating electrifying sets that keep the dancefloor alive. My connection to music goes beyond creation; it's about fostering an experience that brings people together and ignites a shared passion for rhythm and melody. Collaboration is at the heart of my process. I thrive on partnering with artists who share my drive to explore uncharted sonic realms. Every project is an opportunity to blend talents and perspectives, resulting in music that speaks volumes and resonates deeply.
